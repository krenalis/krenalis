# What is Meergo

**Meergo** is a Customer Data Platform (CDP) designed to help teams collect, unify, and activate customer data efficiently and securely. Built with a developer-first approach and native data warehouse compatibility, Meergo empowers you to create flexible data pipelines that fit seamlessly into your existing tech stack.

## What you can do with Meergo

- **Collect customer data**\
  Collect customer data from cloud applications, databases, and data lakes using our prebuilt connectors. 

- **Capture behavioral events**\
  Capture behavioral events from websites, mobile apps, and  backend systems using our SDKs.

- **Store data directly in your warehouse**\
  Store customer data and events directly into your data warehouse — both in batch and real time.

- **Unify customer profiles**\
  Merge data across devices and touchpoints to build a single, accurate view of each customer using identity resolution.

- **Activate insights**\
  Send clean, structured data to your marketing, analytics, and customer engagement tools in real time.

- **Validate with confidence**\
  Ensure your customer data is accurate and consistent with rigorous schema-based validation at both ingestion and delivery stages.

## Who it's for

Meergo CDP is built for engineers, analysts, and growth teams who want full control over their customer data infrastructure without sacrificing ease of use.
