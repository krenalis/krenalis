
PostHog is a product analytics platform that helps teams understand how users interact with their applications. It offers event tracking, funnels, retention and cohort analysis, session recordings, feature flags, and experimentation tools, enabling teams to build better products and make informed, data-driven decisions.

## What can you do with this?

Using this connector you can you send events — for example, those received from your website and mobile app — to PostHog.

## What does it require?

* An account with [PostHog](https://posthog.com/) or a self-hosted installation of PostHog.
