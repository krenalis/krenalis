Brevo is an all-in-one marketing platform that helps businesses engage customers through email, SMS, and WhatsApp campaigns, automate marketing workflows, and manage contacts with built-in segmentation, transactional messaging, and CRM tools.

## What can you do with this?

Using this connector you can read Brevo contacts as users in Meergo, including their core status fields and custom contact attributes.

## What does it require?

* A Brevo account
* A Brevo API key
